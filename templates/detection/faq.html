Frequently Asked Questions

LeafSense is an AI-powered plant disease detection system that helps farmers and gardeners identify plant diseases quickly and accurately. Using advanced machine learning algorithms, it analyzes leaf images to provide instant diagnosis and treatment recommendations.

LeafSense achieves over 95% accuracy in disease detection for common plant diseases. The system is continuously learning and improving its accuracy through user feedback and new data.

LeafSense currently supports a wide range of agricultural crops including tomatoes, potatoes, rice, wheat, corn, and many more. We're constantly expanding our database to include more plant species.

Getting started is easy! Simply create an account, upload a clear photo of the affected plant leaf, and our AI will analyze it instantly. You'll receive detailed information about the disease and recommended treatments.
